Methodology

The Biosecurity Champion Index evaluates every member of Congress across five weighted categories measuring their commitment to securing America's pharmaceutical supply chain and bio-defense posture. Scores are compiled from legislative voting records, committee participation, bill sponsorship, public statements, and appropriations support.

Scores are updated quarterly and reflect the current Congressional session. All data is sourced from official Congressional records, committee transcripts, and publicly available voting data.
